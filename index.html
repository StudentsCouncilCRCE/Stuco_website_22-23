About Us

Get to know us more.

Students’ Council is the apex student body at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College Of Engineering.

The primary duty of the students council comprises of standing strong as a bridge of formal communication between the students, faculties and the administration. While it seeks to address any queries and resolve issues that the students or admin may face, the Student Council also relentlessly strives towards the enrichment of the campus experience.

We aren’t just a group of 21 people working together, rather a family of well coordinated and vividly talented individuals who take pride in the Student Council. Our hearts all beat for a single purpose which is, “To serve and protect the honour and integrity of the council, always.”

StuCo stands stronger!
